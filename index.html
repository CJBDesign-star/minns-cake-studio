Good to Know

Frequently Asked

How is the investment calculated?

A Live Artistry & Service Fee tailored to your venue and logistics (averaging $300), plus $10 per guest for their individual cake tins. The base fee covers transport, setup and live styling. Custom upgrades are quoted on request.

Do guests take the tins home or eat them there?

Completely up to your guests! Tins are served ready to eat on the spot, but come with a matching lid, so guests can pop it on and take their custom creation home for later.

Is this a self serve station?

No, it's a fully hosted experience. I'm behind the cart as your cake artist, piping the buttercream, drizzling the sauces and placing toppings live for every single order.

Is there a minimum guest count?

To bring the full live artistry experience, we require a minimum investment equivalent to 50 guests. You're welcome to book for smaller, more intimate counts provided this baseline is met.

How much space does the cart need?

The cart is 48"L × 25"W with side extenders, and the umbrella stands 7ft tall.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, a permitted home bakery with full event liability insurance. I'm always happy to provide business documentation or issue a custom COI for your venue.

What happens if it rains or gets very hot?

For outdoor bookings I require a viable backup plan to protect the buttercream and handcrafted wooden cart. If rain is forecast or temperatures exceed 85°F, we'll move to a covered or climate controlled space. Fully outdoor venues need a professional pop up canopy for shade and shelter.

What if my guest count changes after booking?

RSVPs fluctuate, so you can adjust your count up until 14 days before the event, when kitchen ordering is finalized. Counts can increase anytime; if final attendance drops, I'll still prepare the full contracted tins so you take the sweet extras home.
